RICE AND MINCE RECIPE

Description

savoury mince is usually a warm and hearty dinner – and was a regular on the table for me growing up in rural Australia. Tender mince cooked with colourful crisp vegetables, thickened just enough to hold its shape when spooned over rice or on thick buttered toast. The word comfort doesn’t do it justice. And it’s not the only retro mince recipe making a comeback.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Brown Onion, finely chopped
  • 500g Beef Mince
  • Gravy Powder
  • 1 cup Tomato puree
  • Potatoes
  • 150g Carrots,mushrooms,peas and corn
  • Mince Meat
  • Steamed rice, to serve
